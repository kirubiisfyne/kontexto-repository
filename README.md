# Kontexto

### Welcome to Kontexto
*Kontexto* is an immersive, narrative-driven editing and campus life simulator. Step into the shoes of a student navigating the intricate world of text editing, assignments, and campus relationships. 

Whether you're picking up print jobs, consulting with your Adviser, or desperately trying to get that perfect score on your latest submission, every word counts and every interaction matters.

### 📝 Gameplay
Explore the campus, talk to a cast of unique NPCs, and take on various writing and editing tasks. When it's time to work, you'll dive into an interactive Text Editor to complete your assignments.

Submit your work to your Adviser and receive **dynamic, real-time grading feedback**. Did you nail the formatting? Did you miss a crucial detail? Your grade—and the way NPCs react to you—will reflect your performance!

### ✨ Key Features
* **Engaging Text-Editing Mechanics:** Jump into a fully custom UI text editor to complete print jobs and format documents. 
* **Dynamic Grading System:** Your work is actively evaluated! Receive detailed feedback based on your performance, from passing grades to perfect scores.
* **Campus Exploration:** Wander a 3D campus environment and discover new tasks and story bits at your own pace.
* **Rich Dialogue & Characters:** Meet characters like Penny and your Adviser. Our robust dialogue system means NPCs remember what tasks you've completed and will update their conversations based on your progress.
* **Seamless Progress:** A reliable auto-save system remembers your exact location, what tasks you've handed in, and the relationships you've built.

### ⌨️ Controls
* **Movement:** WASD / Arrow Keys
* **Interact/Talk:** E (or Left Click)
* **Navigate UI/Editor:** Mouse & Keyboard
